## THE SCRIPT (post-ready, primary safety hook + body + CTA)

We raised $20M to replace every data broker.

Introducing Minerva, the world's first intelligence engine for consumer marketing, powered by proprietary intelligence on 270 million Americans.

Minerva creates a customer profile for every American adult, builds a marketing campaign targeting the best prospects it finds for your brand, and runs everything while you sleep.

Let us show you how powerful Minerva is.

Let's say you're a VP of Marketing at a travel brand trying to drive more bookings.

First, we'll connect Minerva to Stripe, Shopify, and our CRM.

Right now, Minerva is deploying 7 data agents to scan every record from the past five years, building one clean intelligence profile for every customer, including the ones your CRM has never seen. Each profile shows a customer's life stage, household income, browse intent, and next likely purchase.

Now Minerva gets to work for you.

Here, Minerva flags 600 of your existing customers who just had a life event that could change buying behavior: a new baby, a new home, or a marriage.

Minerva builds a re-engagement campaign targeting these customers automatically. With one click you approve, and the campaign goes live.

But Minerva doesn't just work on customers you already have. It also finds the ones you don't.

We'll tell Minerva: "Find me high-wealth travelers who browsed luxury in the last 30 days but never converted."

Minerva scans 270 million Americans, applies your brand filters, and returns 7,000 real people with names, emails, and intent signals, ready to push to Meta, email, and your CRM.

But Minerva does not wait to be asked. While you sleep, it scores your customer base against wealth signals and browse intent. Here, it surfaces 400 customers your CRM never flagged as high-value, creates an upsell campaign, and asks for your approval to launch.

Dozens of brands use Minerva already, like the NBA, Experian, and Hard Rock.

Our CTO built Citadel's data engine, the same one used by Wall Street. Now it runs your marketing stack.

This is what a complete view of your customer looks like.

To celebrate our launch, we're giving 1000 free Minerva credits.
Comment "Minerva" and I'll send you the link.
