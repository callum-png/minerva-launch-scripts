### TYPE 1 — Standard Raise + World's First (Always write this type)
Template: "We raised $[X] to [violent verb] [enemy]. Introducing [Product], the world's first [category] that [bold outcome]. Let me show you how it works."

**1. WithCoverage**
"We raised a $42M Series B led by Sequoia and Khosla to replace every insurance broker. Introducing WithCoverage: The first AI enabled Risk Management Team that outperforms any insurance expert. Let me show you how it works."
*Why it works: $42M + Sequoia/Khosla = credibility. "Replace every insurance broker" = violent verb + named enemy. "Outperforms any insurance expert" = bold measurable claim.*

**2. DepthFirst**
"We raised an $80M Series B led by Meritech to secure the world's software before it's too late. Introducing depthfirst: the world's first security system built for the age of AI."
*Why it works: "Before it's too late" = urgency/stakes. "For the age of AI" = positions in a moment in time.*

**3. Durable**
"We raised $20M dollars to kill the 9-5. Introducing Durable. The first AI business builder that can make anyone more money than a traditional job. Let me show you how it works."
*Why it works: "Kill the 9-5" = enemy is a CONCEPT everyone understands. Incredibly bold.*

**4. Marpipe**
"We raised 10 million dollars to kill UGC ads. Introducing Marpipe, the world's first platform to create, test and customize the world's most profitable ad type. Catalog ads."
*Why it works: "Kill UGC ads" = attacks a specific, well-known format. "Catalog ads" reveal is unexpected.*

**5. Arcads**
"I'm proud to announce that Arcads has raised a 16M dollar seed round. Arcads is the world's first AI ad maker that can make better ads than real creators 10x cheaper and 100x faster."
*Why it works: "Better ads than real creators" = bold comparison. "10x cheaper and 100x faster" = two specific multipliers back to back.*

### TYPE 2 — Creative Visual Hook (Product demonstrates something impossible)

**6. Arcads (Primary)**
"This background you're looking at? Not real. I'm not in a grassy field. I'm not in New York City. And I'm not in your mom's house. It's all fake. Hi, my name is Romain Torres and I'm the founder of Arcads — We're the world's first AI ad maker that actually works. I just told Arcads to make me Asian. Let us show you how powerful Arcads is."

"Can you tell which of these decks was made by AI? If you guessed both were made by AI you're right. Well… kind of. Both were made by humans and AI agents working together."

"Which ad cost $100 and which cost $1? [Clock ticking] Turns out both cost $1 with AI. In fact, I got 10,000 ads and saved $1,000,000. Did you notice that I'm also AI?"
*Why it works: Price comparison → twist → bigger number → second twist. Triple escalation in 4 lines.*
