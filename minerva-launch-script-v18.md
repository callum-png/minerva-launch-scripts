## THE SCRIPT (post-ready, Hook A primary + full body + CTA)

**JACKSON:**
Nobody got into marketing to stare at a screen, waiting for results. But that is what most marketing teams do every day.

We raised $20M to give them the best part of their job back.

**MJ:**
In a world full of AI noise, we knew that a precise understanding of the consumer would be one of the most valuable assets a marketing team could have.

**JACKSON:**
So we built two things: an intelligence layer that creates the right context, and the agents that turn that context into outcomes across the funnel.

**JACKSON:**
Today we are launching Minerva, an AI-native marketing platform powered by proprietary consumer intelligence on 270 million Americans.

**JACKSON:**
We are also announcing partnerships with two of the most important companies in our world.

**MJ:**
OpenAI, where we are doing frontier research on agentic data science and engineering.

**JACKSON:**
And Experian, extending the reach of our consumer context throughout the Fortune 500.

**MJ:**
I spent years at Citadel turning alternative data into alpha for trading equities. Web traffic, consumer spending patterns, life events. Everything that moved consumer behavior, I tracked.

**JACKSON:**
Minerva generates that same kind of alpha for marketing teams, through better data and the agents that act on it.

**JACKSON:**
Let's say you run a sports franchise trying to win back fans who came to one game and never returned.

**MJ:**
Meet David, your AI marketing teammate. First, David connects to every tool you run: your CRM, your ticketing platform, your ad accounts. All of it unified in hours, not weeks.

**JACKSON:**
David reviews every campaign you have ever run and shows you what is driving outcomes. Here, he found that fans who attended a playoff game in the last 18 months are your highest-converting audience, but your current campaigns are not targeting them.

**MJ:**
So David builds the next campaign for you, targeting the right audience with the right message across the right channel. With one click, you approve and the campaign goes live.

**JACKSON:**
But David does not wait to be asked. He scores your entire fan base overnight. Here, he surfaces 600 fans who just had a life event in the last 30 days: a new baby, a new home, a marriage. He builds the re-engagement campaign and asks for your approval to launch.

**MJ:**
Results flow back, the platform learns, and every campaign is sharper than the last.

**JACKSON:**
The NBA, Hard Rock Stadium Group, Wander, Trust and Will, and Luxury Presence already use Minerva to drive measurable results.

This is what it looks like when your marketing team never sleeps.

**JACKSON:**
To celebrate our launch, we are giving 1,000 free Minerva credits. Comment "Minerva" and I will send you the link.
