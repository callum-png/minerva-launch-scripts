## THE SCRIPT (post-ready, primary safety hook + body + CTA)

We raised $20M to replace the marketing agency.
Introducing Minerva, the world's first AI for consumer marketing powered by proprietary intelligence on 270 million Americans.
Let us show you how powerful Minerva is.

Let's say you're a VP of Marketing at a travel brand trying to drive more bookings.

First, we'll connect Minerva to Stripe, Shopify, and HubSpot.
Minerva deploys data agents to scan every record from the past five years, building one clean profile of your customers, including people not in your CRM.

Minerva flags 600 of your customers who just hit a life event: a new baby, a new home, a marriage.
Minerva asks to build a campaign to re-engage these customers.
You approve.

But Minerva doesn't just work on customers you already have. It also finds the ones you don't.

We'll tell Minerva: "Find me high-wealth travelers who browsed luxury in the last 30 days but never converted."
Minerva returns 7,000 real people, ready to push to Meta, email, and your CRM in one click.

Minerva doesn't wait to be asked, scoring your base against wealth signals and browse intent to surface 400 customers your CRM never flagged as high-value and ask to run the campaign.

Dozens of brands use Minerva: the NBA, Experian, and Hard Rock among them.

Our CTO built Citadel's data engine. Now it runs your marketing stack.

This is what a complete view of your customer looks like.

To celebrate launch we're giving 1000 free Minerva credits.
Comment "Minerva" and I'll send you the link.
